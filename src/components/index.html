Why AI?

Reduces Operational Costs

Implementing AI reduces the need for extensive human resources and can significantly lower operational costs.
With Ms. Kalinga, you can achieve high-quality news delivery without the overhead costs associated with
traditional news anchors.

Adaptable and Scalable

AI solutions like Ms. Kalinga are highly scalable and adaptable to your specific needs. Whether you are a small local
station or a large international network, AI can easily scale to meet your
requirements and grow with your organization.

Reach a Global Audience

Ms. Kalinga can deliver news in multiple languages, breaking down language barriers and allowing you to reach a
broader, more diverse audience. This multilingual capability helps expand your reach and connect with viewers
around the world.

Tailored to Your Brand


AI anchors can be customized to match your brand's identity. From appearance to delivery style, Ms. Kalinga can
be tailored to resonate with your audience and align with your brand's values and aesthetics.
